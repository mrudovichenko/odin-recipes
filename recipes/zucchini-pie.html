Zucchini Pie

Description

Delicious zucchini bake with enough to feed two people over two nights. Can substitute mushroom for bacon to make it vegetarian. Add Sriracha for a little extra zing!

Ingredients

  • 4 large zucchini
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 bacon rashers
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 4 eggs
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Grate unpeeled zucchini, chop onion finely, chop bacon.
  2. Combine zucchini, onion, bacon, cheese, flour, oil and beaten egg.
  3. Season salt and pepper. Pour into greased flan dish. Back 30-40 mins in med oven (180 degrees C).
