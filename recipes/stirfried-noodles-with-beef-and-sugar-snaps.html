Stir-Fried Noodles with Beef and Sugar Snaps

Description

Stir-fried noodles with beef and sugar snaps. Delicious with brown rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1.5 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 3 tablespoons chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon light-flavoured oil
  • 400g beef fillet or rump, sliced
  • 4cm piece of fresh ginger, julienned or grated
  • 200g sugar snap peas

    • To serve

  • 250g fresh egg noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

  1. Stir together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, sherry, stock and sugar in a small bowl
  2. Heat a wok or large frying pan over high heat. Add the oil and, when smoking, stir-fry the beef in two batches, cooking for 1 minute until it is sealed and browned. Remove from the wok and set aside. Stir-fry the ginger and peas for 2 minutes, adding a little more oil if needed. Add the beef and sauce and cook for 1 minute, until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  3. Divide the noodles among four bowls and top with the stir-fry. (Or add the noodles to the wok and mix it all together.) Serves 4 adults or lots of children.
